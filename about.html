About WSRTBD

Protecting Wildlife, Saving Lives

Who We Are

Wildlife and Snake Rescue Team Bangladesh (WSRTBD) is the largest snake and wildlife rescue organization in Bangladesh. Founded with a mission to protect both wildlife and communities, we have been serving across all divisions of Bangladesh.

Our team consists of trained and certified rescuers who work 24/7 to respond to emergency calls, rescue snakes and other wildlife from human-habited areas, and safely relocate them to their natural habitats.

We believe in coexistence and work tirelessly to educate communities about wildlife conservation and safe practices when encountering wild animals.

Our Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To protect wildlife and ensure safe coexistence between humans and animals through rescue, rehabilitation, and community education.

Our Vision

A Bangladesh where wildlife thrives in harmony with human communities, and every creature is valued and protected.

Our Values

Compassion, professionalism, community service, conservation, and dedication to both wildlife welfare and human safety.
